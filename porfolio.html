Multimedia designer with 5+ Years Experience

I am a passionate multimedia designer who creates engaging visual and interactive content by combining graphic design, animation, video, and digital media. I use my creativity and technical skills to develop designs for websites, mobile apps, social media, advertisements, and presentations.
